"IdeaHub" is a web-based project by two Washington University in St. Louis students based on the belief that everyone should be able to pursue their dream. By connecting those with ideas to developers looking for work, IdeaHub looks to become the number one resource to find dedicated teams to develop projects and brilliant ideas waiting to be created.

If you are interested in becoming a part of IdeaHub, or looking for more information, please contact us at admin@______.com. We're very excited about this project and are looking for potential users or investors who feel the same. If you are interested in becoming a part of IdeaHub whether as a developer, user, or investor, or are simply looking for more information, please contact us at admin@____.com. We plan to have a working prototype ready and available by the end of the year. At that time we will begin inviting interested users and investors to try our platform and prepare the service for a public launch.
